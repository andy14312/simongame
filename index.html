Simon Game!

Rules and Instructions

  • Game presents you a series of light flashes. After the game shows you those, you gotta follow them by clicking
  • As soon as you complete the trail correctly, you're given the completed trail and a next light flash which then again you gotta follow
  • As soon as you complete 20 light flashes, you're the winner
  • You can also increase the game intensity from 20 to what you think you're capable of
  • In non-strict mode once you break the sequence of light flashes, you're reminded of the correct sequence to follow after which you can try again
  • In strict mode, once you break the sequence, you're game is done and you loose. You can restart the game to play from start
